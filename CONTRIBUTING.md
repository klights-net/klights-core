klights-core, you agree that your contribution is licensed under the same
dual-license model as the project:

- AGPL-3.0-or-later for open-source use; and

If you cannot agree to these terms, do not submit the contribution.

## Developer Certificate Of Origin

Each commit should include a `Signed-off-by` line, using your real name or the
identity you use for contribution records:

```text
Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
```

The sign-off means you certify that you wrote the contribution or otherwise have
the right to submit it under this project's contribution license.
